## Mismatch root-cause analysis (NYU) — eval-protocol, not model

Re-scored the **same** 654 cached CUT3R predictions (no re-inference) against NYU GT
under 4 protocol variants × 2 GT fields (`scripts/ablate_nyu_gtfield.py`):

| Variant | raw GT | filled GT |
|---|---|---|
| A crop + clip **[plumbline]** | **0.0522** | 0.0605 |
| D no-crop, no-clip **[CUT3R `eval_metrics.py`]** | 0.0526 | **0.0777** |

(Variant A on raw reproduces the live run's 0.0522 exactly → methodology validated.)

**Source = GT depth field (dominant) + Eigen crop (secondary):**
1. **raw vs filled GT (+0.025):** plumbline pins `depth_field="raw"` (sparse Kinect);
   CUT3R's eval loads `np.load(data/nyu/*.npy)` = DUSt3R-lineage **dense/filled** depth.
   Under CUT3R's own protocol, raw→filled moves 0.0526→0.0777.
2. **Eigen crop (−0.017):** plumbline applies it; `eval_metrics.py` applies no spatial
   crop (`gt>0` only). On filled GT the crop drops noisier interpolated borders.
3. **Residual 0.0777→0.086 (~10%):** exact NYU image set (their `.npy` vs our 654
   `.mat` Eigen indices) + `cv2.INTER_CUBIC` vs PIL-bilinear resize.

NOT the cause: alignment (both median scale-only) and abs_rel formula (identical) —
proven since raw + CUT3R-protocol still yields 0.0526.

KITTI / Bonn are the same class (eval-set/selection differences): KITTI plumbline
Eigen-652+Garg vs CUT3R `val_selection_cropped` (1000, no crop); Bonn plumbline 8
seqs/all-frames vs CUT3R 5 seqs (balloon2/crowd2/crowd3/person_tracking2/synchronous)
× 110 frames, max_depth=70.

**Conclusion:** the CUT3R cells are an eval-protocol mismatch, not a model/inference
bug — plumbline's `nyu_eigen_2014` raw+crop default is stricter than the DUSt3R-lineage
dense+no-crop protocol these papers report. Fix: add a DUSt3R-lineage NYU variant
(`depth_field=filled`, no crop) for CUT3R/MonST3R/π³ cells, or document in
`docs/DISCREPANCIES.md`.
